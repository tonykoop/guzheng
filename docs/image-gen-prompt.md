# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished guzheng — a long plucked board zither — presented as a real, hand-built physical object. The instrument is a slender, gently arched wooden soundboard over a hollow resonating body, far longer than it is wide, with a softly curved crown rising along its length and tapering toward a rounded "Phoenix" tail end and a fuller "Dragon" head end. A full course of strings runs the length of the top from a string rest at one end to a tuning-box and peg area at the other, and the signature feature is a row of individual movable bridges — slim inverted-Y "pillar" supports standing upright in a single staggered diagonal line down the soundboard, each lifting one string clear of the top. Materials read as warm figured paulownia or similar light tonewood for the soundboard with a darker hardwood rim, side boards, and end blocks, a hand-rubbed satin finish, and pale taut strings catching the light. Realistic tabletop scale cues, clean neutral light-grey studio background, soft directional studio light, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The same guzheng staged in a quiet instrument-maker's workshop on a low wooden stand or bench, angled so the diagonal row of movable bridges and the gentle soundboard crown are visible. Soft natural window light rakes across the top, warm sawdust-and-wood-shaving atmosphere, hand tools and a crown-measurement fixture softly out of focus in the background, shallow depth of field, environmental storytelling, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the signature movable-bridge corridor: a few slim upright bridge "pillars" standing on the arched soundboard, each cradling a single taut string in its grooved top, casting crisp shadows under low raking light. Show the contrast of pale tonewood grain, the polished bridge feet meeting the crowned top, and the fine strings receding out of focus along the corridor. High detail, tactile material texture, no measurements, no callouts.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, fake maker marks, no dimension lines or callouts, no tuning numbers, no impossible mechanisms or floating parts; bridges sit plausibly on the top, strings stay straight and continuous; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
